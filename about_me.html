A Few Facts About Myself

Born: Champaign-Urbana, Illinois.

I am currently a student at Great Bay community college studying Computer Technologies.

My interest in computers stems from a fundamental interest in creation. Humans as a race are defined by their great faculties of creation and consumption. I noticed early on that I increasingly the one consuming the fruits of technology, rather than contributing to it. It was with this realization mind that I discovered a love for creation.

I make things with computers

My love of creation is expressed via many outlets. My interests include:

  • Web Development
  • Computer Programming
  • Photography
  • Graphic Design
  • Music

The Long-term

So, what's my end-game? What am I hoping to do at the end of the day, my life's goal, my dream job?

I want to become an active participant in a new generation of human computer interaction. A generation dominated by wearable computers, virtual reality, augmented reality, and responsive, responsible, interactive and most of all intuitive design. It is for this purpose that I am enrolled at Great Bay community college, and it is for this purpose that I will be attending the University of New Hampshire in the fall to pursue a degree in computer science. I plan on approaching the issue of human-computer interaction from a background well-versed in computer science, so that I can not only design, but also implement effective human-computer interaction.
